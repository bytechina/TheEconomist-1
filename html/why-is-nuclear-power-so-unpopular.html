Nuclear power
Why is nuclear power so unpopular?

Nuclear power is one of the safest and most sustainable forms of energy, but it only constitutes 10% of global electricity generation

Economist Films
Films

TEN YEARS ago the meltdown at a nuclear power plant in Fukushima, Japan, stoked anxieties about nuclear power, which was already unpopular. The number of active reactors has barely changed since the 1980s. Yet nuclear energy is safe, reliable and produces zero emissions. This film traces its history and the reasons why it is not more widely used around the world.
