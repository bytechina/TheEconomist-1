What if America reverts to abortion bans?

Anne McElvoy talks to Mary Ziegler, a ​​legal historian, about the origins of Roe v Wade and what will happen if the landmark legislation is overturned

FOR 50 YEARS, women in America have had a constitutional right to terminate a pregnancy. Now, a leaked draft opinion suggests that the Supreme Court will overturn Roe v Wade. Anne McElvoy asks Mary Ziegler, a legal historian, about the origins of the landmark legislation and what would happen if Roe is cast aside. Plus, does the Supreme Court need reforming?
