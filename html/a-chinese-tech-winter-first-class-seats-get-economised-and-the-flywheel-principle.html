Money talks
A Chinese tech winter, first-class seats get economised and the flywheel principle

Our weekly podcast on the markets, the economy and the world of business

A TECH winter is coming. In this week’s episode of “Money talks” we look at how a once white-hot tech sector in China is shedding capital, employees and bonuses and heading for a freeze. We also discuss the private-jet industry and why airlines are phasing out first-class seats. And Jim Collins, author of “Good to Great”, explains the flywheel principle. Simon Long hosts. Runtime: 18 min.
