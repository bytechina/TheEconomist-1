Our stories on the potential for, and possible impact of, conflict in eastern Europe

OVER 100,000 Russian troops are massed on Ukraine’s borders, in apparent preparation for a two-front attack that would divide Ukrainian forces and menace the capital, Kyiv. The odds of war seem perilously high, but no one can be sure of Vladimir Putin’s intentions.
