How do you solve a problem like malaria?

Our podcast on the science and technology making the news. This week we examine new ways to fight the deadly disease, from vaccines to genetically modified mosquitoes

SQUASHING MALARIA could, over the next three decades, save as many lives as covid-19 has taken. We explore new ways to fight infections: from the introduction of the first malaria vaccines, to genetically modified mosquitoes. What would it take to vanquish one of the world’s deadliest diseases? Alok Jha hosts. Runtime: 39 min
